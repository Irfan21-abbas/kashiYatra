Kashi Yatra from Bangalore by flight including Gaya and Allahabad

Duration

5 Days / 4 Nights

Price

30,000 Rupees per person

Travel Mode

From/To Bangalore

Day 1

09:15 am - Departure from Bangalore to Varanasi

11:50 am - Arrival at Varanasi Airport

12:15 pm - Pickup from Airport and proceed to hotel (standard checkin- 12:00 pm)-fresh up

03:30 pm - proceed for darshan of Shri Kashi Viswanath, Shri Vishalakshi, Annapurni

05:00 pm - Proceed for Ganga Aarti. Night stay at Kashi

Day 2

05:00 am - Proceed for rituals at ganga

08:30 am - Breakfast

12:30 pm - Check out and proceed to Gaya. Visit Maha Bodhi tree and Buddha temple. Night stay at Gaya

Day 3

07:00 am - Proceed for rituals at Vishnu Padam.

On completion, have lunch

Then proceed to Varanasi. Stay at Varanasi

Day 4

09:00 am - Breakfast

Then Proceed to Allahabad

Then Visit Triveni Sangamam, Shri Hanuman Temple, Anand Bhavan etc

Proceed to Varanasi. Night stay at Varanasi

Day 5

10:00 am - Checkout from the hotel and Proceed to Airport. On the way Lunch.

01:45 pm Departing from Varanasi to Bangalore
